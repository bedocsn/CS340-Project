Dangers

ID Danger Name Description Difficulty Rating Edit/Delete
1 Poisonous Hornet Swarm An ever-growing cloud of hornets has been angered and plagues the encounter. Starts as a small circular boundaries that can move around the play space and anything that crosses into that boundaries takes 2 damage on entering and 2 additional every turn until they exit the circle. Each full turn, the size of the swarm grows. 5
2 Acid Tornado A tornado that can move 1 general move distance per full turn. The direction is picked at random via dice roll. Players caught in its path take poison damage and are violently thrown two move distances away in a random direction and take additional collision and landing damage. 6
3 Collapsing Ceiling The entire structure or cave is collapsing and large chunks are falling down. Players can do a check the turn prior to see where the weakest points are for where the next chunks will fall, otherwise the chunks will fall at random that turn if no check was made. If a chunk hits a player, they take 20 damage. 4
4 Traps Everywhere Players need to tread lightly and make observation checks since the entire placed is rigged. Tripwires the cause darts to fire, tiles that trigger spikes to pop out of the ground, and levers rigged to shoot poison gas if the incorrect code combo is riddle is entered all abound. 5
5 Ticking Time Bomb At the center of the encounter, a steaming, pulsing piece of ancient looking magical technology coughs and sputters. Arcs of unstable magical electricity shoot out and arc back into the mystery device. It is making a ticking sound and small clock seems to be noting a count down. In 5 turns this thing is going to explode. If it explodes, the GM can pick the effect (ie massive damage in a certain radius, a swarm of disease-carrying hyper mosquitos swarm out, sticky super-glue-like magical lava liquid sprays everywhere, etc). There is also a code on the device, and wires in the back written in ancient elvish text. 7
6 Toxic Liquid Rising Every turn, toxic liquid seeping from the ground or leaks in the walls continues to spill out, raising the level of the liquid each turn. As a result, the places where players can walk without incurring poison damage and effects diminishes as they need to climb higher and higher to avoid the ever-rising miasma. 3
7 Flame Geiser Every 3 turns, a spire or multiple spires will erupt, causing an area of effect damage radius around the spire. Different spires can erupt at different points in time, so players will have to take note of which spires erupt during which turn so they know which ones are safe to be around and which ones are about to pop for that turn and then next. 8
8 Roving Slime Beasts The area is overrun with seemingly docile massive slime beasts that just mill about. However, if they are touched by anything, they will fly into a violent, wild rage and attack anything around them that isn't a sludge beast. 7
9 Spiked Walls Closing In The players are surrounded by an enclosure that has sharp walls that cause 10 damage when touched. Every turn they close inward, diminished the area everything can move around. The players need to escape or solve the encounter before they are crushed by the walls. 5
10 Camouflaged Jump-Scare Stalkers Players must make checks on the objects around them (trees, tables, chests, rocks, etc, depending on the location) as it may not in fact be an object, but a magically camouflaged enemy that can get a surprise attack on unsuspecting players. 9
11 Massive Lurching Machinery Enormous gears turn and large metallic switches and levers click and fall into place as some sort of ancient and mysterious piece of thousand-year-old dwarven machinery continues to function long after it had been abandoned. Players must take note of the movements and patterns of the huge, lurching machinery all around them as they can be knocked down off a ledge or crunched into gears if they aren't careful. 6
12 Large War Party of Goblins A group of Goblins 5x the size of the player group, armed with crude, rusty blades and simple crossbows, are hostile towards the player group. Players must defeat them, or maybe find a secret weakness they posses, or, against all odds, try to negotiate. 3
13 Sleeping Colossus A super-massive being sleeps in the center of the encounter. It guards what the players have been seeking to advance. There is a possibility of advancing without having to fight this terrifying boss foe, but players will have to be smart, daring, and crafty, and the risk of waking it up and having to endure a harrowing battle is a constant. 10
14 Enraged Basilisk An aggressive basilisk seeks to eat all players in the encounter. The massive boss creature is capable of temporary flight, multiple ranged and melee attacks per turn, and heals itself at the end of every full turn. A truly formidable foe. 10
15 Dark Magic Poltergeists A group of Poltergeists 3x the size of the player group, armed with ranged magical spells that can cause lots of damage and small area-of-effect damage on impact attack the players. They are also capable of flying. Even if you survive their attacks, getting hit by one of these unstable magical attacks can lead to a character losing all their hair, or permanently changing their voice, or requiring glasses from there on out. 8
