Welcome to QuestLog, a tool for Game Masters to track numerous important aspects of their game!

With QuestLog, you can:
- Log your Players
- Log your Players's various Heroes
- Manually or Randomly Generate and Modify Encounters
- Input new Dangers to populate future Encounters
- Maintain a history of Heroes' Accomplishments

Please select an option from above to create, read, update, or delete an in game entity.
As always remember to have fun and be respectful of the other players.
